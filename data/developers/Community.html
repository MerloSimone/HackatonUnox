recipes.program -
if it is an EVEREO recipe:

  • "cat": Food category
  • "hum": Humidity in the device
  • "temp": Temperature of food holding
  • "regen": Duration of the pasteurization
  • "holding": Duartion of food holding

else:
  • "steps": an array of
    • "fan": Fan speed (1 to 5)
    • "temp": Temperature of cooking step
    • "time": Duration time of step
    • "clima": Humidity setting (-100, 100), negative value means humidity extraction, positive value means humidity addition using steam
    • "pulse": Define the pulse parameter of the fan, can be 0 or 1
    • "preset": Enumeration of braise, bread, combi, dehydrate, focaccia, fry, grill, hyper_smoker, manual, over_night, pastry, pizza, pressure_steam, roast,s ous_vide, steam. Type of preset choosen from the possible choices
    • "microwave": For speed ovens, it is the percentage of waves ranging from 0 to 100
    • "smokerSize": Either 0 or 1
    • "smoker": Smoking intensity (0 to 10)
    • "delta": In the cooking steps, it is the diffrence between the probe temperature and the chamber temperature.
  • "preheat": an object of
    • "temp": Temperature of preheating
    • "time": Preheating time in seconds
    • "delta": The temperature added to the temperature of the previous step
